Project Description

The project aims to develop a responsive e-commerce website for selling smartphones and accessories.
The website will allow customers to browse through a wide range of smartphones and accessories,
select their desired products, and purchase them using a secure online payment system.
The website will have a user-friendly interface with easy navigation and search options.
Customers will be able to search for their desired products using different
filters such as price range, brand, operating system, screen size, camera quality, and other specifications.
The website will have a separate section for accessories such as phone cases, screen protectors,
chargers, earphones, and other related items.
Customers will also be able to read product descriptions, check reviews, and compare different products before making a purchase.
